## Business problem
The goal is to identify strategic operations decisions a VM owner can make during the early stage of capturing and utilizing their sales data. This project analyzes vending machine (VM) sales data to enable the owner to make informed decisions to increase revenue.

## Data source

- [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/awesomeasingh/vending-machine-sales/data) - Vending Machine Sales

## Tech stack
- Python
- Tableau Public

## Methods

- Exploratory data analysis (EDA)

## Recommendation and lessons learned
Based on the findings, the data recommends that owners should:
* Prioritize capturing weekday sales
* Restock on Sundays, aka the day with the lowest utilization
* Test products and operational plans in Q1 before the spring and summer sales rush
* Continue provideing food and beverage machines while offering payments via cash and credit

## What could be improved
* Perform customer research at the public library and construction site (i.e., Earle Asphalt) for identifying the type of machine and products to provide at similar locations
  * Highest month over month revenue = public library
  * Highest difference in product categories = construction site
* Defining product categories and properly identifying the products among them offers more consistency and trust in our outputs
* Combining this information with operational cost details would offer insights on profitability and operational performance
